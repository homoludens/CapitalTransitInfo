CapitalTransitInfo was built by Kurt Raschke to address outstanding deficiencies in public transit apps for the DC area in a platform-agnostic way.

CapitalTransitInfo uses real-time data from WMATA, DC Circulator, and ART - Arlington Transit.

The application uses Flask, MongoDB, and jQuery Mobile.
